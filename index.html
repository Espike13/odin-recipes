Banana bread

A delicious and simple recipe! We all throw out bananas because we never use them in time, am I right? Well, we have a solution for that. Never waste a banana's precious life again!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 very ripe bananas, peeled and mashed
  • 1/3 cup (76g) butter, unsalted or salted, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups (205g) all-purpose flour
