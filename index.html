Banana bread

Banana bread at work? Hell yeah, dude.

We all throw out bananas because we never use them in time, am I right? Well, we have a solution for that. Never waste a banana's precious life again!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 very ripe bananas, peeled and mashed
  • 1/3 cup (76g) butter, unsalted or salted, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups (205g) all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 and grease your bread pan.
  2. Combine the bananas, butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla into a medium mixing bowl
  3. In a separate large bowl whisk together the baking soda, salt, and flour
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry, and stir until wel incorporated. Don't overmix!
  5. Bake in you preheated oven for 1 hour. Allow to rest and cool before slicing.
  6. Enjoy!
